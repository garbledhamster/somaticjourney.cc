Arch & Flatten

Purpose: Release tension in the lower back and improve spinal mobility.

  1. Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor.
  2. As you inhale, tilt your pelvis forward, arching your lower back.
  3. As you exhale, tilt your pelvis backward, pressing your back into the floor.

Back Lift

Purpose: Release tension in the upper back and shoulders.

  1. Lie on your stomach with your forehead resting on your hands.
  2. Inhale as you lift your head and chest off the floor slightly.
  3. Exhale as you lower back down, relaxing completely.
  4. Repeat for 5â€“7 repetitions, moving slowly and with control.

Purpose: Release diagonal tension and improve coordination.

Purpose: Release rotational tension in the spine.

  1. Lie on your back with knees bent and arms out to the sides.
